Interesting facts

Appetizers

The difference from the USA is that row vegetables and fresh herbs are alvays there, in winter pickled vegetables.

Barbecue

In the beginning of May everyone wants to enjoy first warm days and have an outdoor meal: pork or chicken "shaslik" is done on the metal sticks over the hot wood after burning them. It has different taste from the barbecue, to measure temperature people would cut or eat one piece. Vegetables are made as well, and if the wood is hot enough and the time is not a problem, potato is thrown there for several hours.

New Year

In many families New Year is celebrated more than Christmas.

Salads

Some salads that are considered traditional for New Year taste better next day. In fact, after New Year there is so much leftovers, that the whole next week one can eat them. Because, as it is said, "you will spend a year just as good as you meet it".
